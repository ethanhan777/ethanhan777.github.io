Background story

Teemo is a humanoid robot that has the ability to recognize moving objects, postures, gestures, its surrounding environment, sounds and faces, which enables it to interact with humans for helping mental health issues.

Teemo can detect the movements of multiple objects by using visual information captured by cameras in its head and also determine distance and direction. This feature allows Teemo to follow or face a person when approached.

Teemo interprets voice commands and human gestures, enabling it to recognize when a handshake is offered or when a person waves or points, and then responds accordingly. Teemo’s ability to distinguish between voices and other sounds allows it to identify its companions. Teemo is able to respond to its name and recognizes sounds associated with a falling object or collision.

These features allow Teemo to support any medical needs for people who require it. For example, reminding the time for their medications, fall detection to call an emergency, and connecting to doctors virtually for their regular checkups through teemo’s screen on its chest.
